    ## Why Start This Project?
    The Digital Ocean project originated from the author "ML"'s personal knowledge base. During university, a collection of frequently used resources had accumulated and was shared regularly. However, as the number of shared resources grew without any systematic organization, the knowledge became fragmented and difficult to locate. This project was created to consolidate past information and establish a unified way to share knowledge and collected resources.
    <hr/>

    ## Where Does the Knowledge Come From?
    This project currently draws from three sources: web pages captured from the internet, personal notes, and submitted contributions. Viewers can submit knowledge or resources for the platform team to review; once approved, the content becomes visible on the site. Since much of the knowledge originates from online sources, any information collected from the internet includes a citation link to the original source, with copyright remaining with the original website. This site only organizes and distills key points. Copyright for personal notes and insights belongs to the author, and submitted articles imply consent for the platform to publish the content.
