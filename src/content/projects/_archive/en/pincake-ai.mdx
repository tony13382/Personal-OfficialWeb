    <p className="font-bold">The job-seeking product market falls into three main categories</p>

    <ul className="list-disc space-y-2 mt-4 ps-5">
      <li>`Online Resume Builders`: These tools offer various resume templates and customization features, allowing users to easily create resumes suited to their needs. Common online resume builders include Canva, Visual CV, and CakeResume. Observing from 2023, based on publicly available CakeResume user data, at least 2 million users in Taiwan have used such services, and this market continues to expand.</li>
      <li>`Resume Analysis Tools`: These tools use artificial intelligence or machine learning to analyze resume content and provide suggestions, helping users improve resume quality. Common tools include the resume diagnostics offered by 104 Job Bank and 1111 Job Bank. Observing from 2023, based on publicly available user data from those platforms, at least 100,000 users have used these services and paid to optimize their resumes.</li>
      <li>`AI Mock Interviews`: Most mock interview services are delivered by real people, and are largely tailored to applications for foreign companies or overseas study. In Taiwan, only job posting platforms like job banks offer similar services.</li>
    </ul>
  </CardContent>
